Target Audience

The target audience of Rocky Mountain Weather is described below

  • WHO: Anyone living in the Rocky Mountain Region who needs to know an upcoming forecast.
  • Age: 14 to 100
  • Technology: Anyone with a mobile device or computer
  • Motivation: Desire to know information about local weather patterns
